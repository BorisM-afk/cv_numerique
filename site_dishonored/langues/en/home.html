Welcome to Dunwall

Dishonored is an immersive sim action-adventure series. You play as a supernatural assassin driven by revenge in a plague-ridden steampunk world.

Your choices determine the fate of the world. Will you succumb to violence or find another way?

  • Stealth and Infiltration:

    Choose shadow over light. Sneak behind guards, traverse the rooftops, and complete your missions without ever being spotted to earn the title of "Ghost".

  • Powers of the Void:

    The Mark of the Outsider grants you supernatural abilities. Teleport instantly with Blink, possess your enemies, or freeze time to dominate your opponents.

  • Chaos System:

    Your actions have consequences. A violent and lethal playstyle will increase Chaos, making the city darker, infested with rats and weepers, ultimately altering the story's ending.
